# Zadonetske — at a glance
**Coordinates:** 49.6407° N, 36.3526° E  
**Administration:** Zmiiv urban hromada, Chuhuiv district, Kharkiv region  
**Local form of the name:** Zadinecke (from the local name of the Siverskyi Donets River — Dinec)

The village stretches along the left bank of the Siverskyi Donets and is surrounded by pine forests of the Homilshanski Lisy National Park. Floodplain lakes (including Bile Lake), sandy beaches and scenic viewpoints make it perfect for quiet nature breaks, walks and fishing.

> Note: open sources list the pre‑2020 population at around 690; current figures to be verified.
